Major functions of the Electrical & Maintenance Division

S.No.
Function
1
 To develop & maintain the Power Distribution System, Electrical Installations, HVAC,Audio Visual Systems, Fire fighting systems, solar water heating & solar power Systems.
2
 Taking the meter reading of all the Residential accommodations & Academic buildings across the IIT Bombay campus & generating the bill on a monthly basis.
3
 Sending the segregated metering information to the different divisions at IIT Bombay.
4
 Complaint registration by the occupants of the existing buildings at IIT Bombay and the rectification of the same.
5
 Taking the request for Procuring A/C from different division of IIT Bombay and issuing of the same.
6
 Maintenance of the A/C's already fitted at different locations on a regular basis.
