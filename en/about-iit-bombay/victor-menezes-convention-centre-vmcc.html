The Institute also has a large auditorium (Convocation Hall) with a seating capacity of 1400.  The rental for the Convocation Hall follows:

  1. Convocation Hall charge Rs. 20000 (with AV) + AC charges Rs. 2000 per hours. (Half-day)
  2. Convocataion Hall charge Rs. 25000 (with AV) + AC charge Rs. 2000 per hours. (Full-day)
