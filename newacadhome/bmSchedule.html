Biometric Validation and ID Card Generation

UG students provisionally admitted through JEE (Advanced) or UCEED have to validate their biometric fingerprint captured during the examination, as per the schedule given below. Only upon successful valiation they will be issued an IIT Bombay ID card. Please follow the dress code given below for the ID Card photo capture.

Please bring a printed copy of the following documents:

  1. For JEE: JoSAA provided Provisional Offer/Admssion Letter
  2. For UCEED: UCEED Hall ticket
  3. IIT Bombay temporary Visitor pass (that contains the barcode)
You need not bring the handfilled ID card form, given to you in your orientation folder.

ID Card Photo Specifications

Your photograph captured now will be the one for all Institute records for the next four or more years and may be used for your graduation convocation video live streamed on the net. So dress up well and be kind on your appearance.

  1. Your tops must be of nearly uniform colour of medium to dark shades. The background will be white, so avoid whites and off-white tops.
  2. Formal attire (like a blazer) is acceptable, but not needed. Definitely do not make it fancy or multi-coloured or festive wear.
  3. Full face must be visible without any masks or covering.
  4. Hairline above the forehead must be visible.
