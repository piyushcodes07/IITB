Frequently Asked Questions

  1. What are the programmes of study at IIT Bombay


    2. The Academic programmes of study at IIT are categorised into three sections as UnderGraduate Programs, PostGraduate Programs and Research Programs.

    (for detailed information either click on the Admissions link on the main menu.

  2. How do I apply for Admissions


    3. Admissions to various courses at IITB is given on the performance of the student in the respective common test and interviews being conducted.For example : Admissions to M.Tech goes through GATE , B.Tech through JEE, MSc. through Test & Interview and so on.

    (for detailed information click on the Admissions link on the main menu provided to the left hand side of the webpage)

  3. What are the research opportunities available for UnderGraduate and PostGraduate students


    4. For UnderGraduate students we have something called URA. you can get information on this on the main menu of Academic Homepage.
