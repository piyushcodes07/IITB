Swar Vichar
  • Motivation

    The Cell for Human values at IITB is an effort at sensitizing IITB students to some of the non-technical opportunities of fulfillment.

    Under this programme we have invited Mrs. Veena Sahasrabuddhe who is one of the most eminent artists of Hundustaniclassical music, to interact with students of IITB through a programme called "SWAR VICHAAR".The emphasis of this programme is on increasing music appreciation rather than creating professional artists.


  • Time & Duration

    1. From the last week of July to October end
    2. From January to the first week of April
