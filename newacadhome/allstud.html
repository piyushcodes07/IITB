Interface to update Email
This interface allows the all students to change their primary email address. Students should be careful while entering the new one as it would be the one where all communication from the Online system will be done.


Online-Registration by Students   
This interface allows the students to do their on-line course registration for the current semester.


Check your registration status
This interface allows the students to view their registration status, whether it has been approved, disapproved or un-processed.


Online-Course Evaluation by Studentss   
This interface allows the students to do their on-line course evaluation for a particular Course.
