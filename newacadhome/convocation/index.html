The soft-copy of the transcript and final degree certificates of all students, who will be conferred degrees in the Main Session of the 60th Convocation of IIT Bombay will be uploaded on the portal [cvl.nad.co.in]. Students can view/ download the same, subject to the completion of no-dues-clearance, 05th September, 2022.

The students who are viewing/receiving their Degree certificate/Transcript with "question mark" with a remark "Signature Not Verified" need to "validate" the digital signature. The link for Validating Digital Signature in Adobe is given below.
