Admission to International Students for Ph.D. and Postgraduate Programmes for the Academic year (2022-23)

Anyone who is not a national of India will be considered as an international student for the purpose of admission and fees.

Admission in Ph.D. program for foreign nationals will be made twice a year and admission in Postgraduate program for foreign nationals will be offered once a year.

Admission Application Deadlines
M.Tech., M.B.A., M.Des, etc. and Ph.D. (Admission for Autumn Semester) 1 January - 31 March (every year)
Ph.D. Programme (Admission for Spring Semester) 1 September - 31 October (every year)
