Postgraduate Programmes

Admission to Postgraduate programmes at IIT Bombay is through following :
  • GATE (Graduate Aptitude Test in Engineering) for M.Tech. programmes

  • CEED(Common Entrance Exam for Design) for M.Des. programmes

  • MET (M.Phil. Entrance Test) for M.Phil. programme

  • JAM(Joint Admission Test) for M.Sc.

  • CAT(Common Admission Test) for M.Mgt.
