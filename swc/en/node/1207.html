Vartalap- Let's Discuss

Mind That Matters
Event Date: 
Saturday, 14 August 2021 - 3:00pm to 4:15pm

The Core Counselling Team of Student Wellness Centre, feels proud to invite you to "Vartalap: Let's Discuss", a series of discussions around various topics related to Demystifying Mental Health. 

 

Details are as follows:

Topic: Mind That Matters

Date: 14 August, 2021

Time: 3.00 pm

Duration: 1 hr

Speaker: Dr Rajendra Barve (Psychiatrist, IIT Bombay)

Event Link: : https://kaksha.webex.com/kaksha/onstage/g.php?MTID=e099888c01b937feeac58cff718ce8c4b

Event Access Code: 170 388 5539

Event Password: 112233

 

We encourage everyone to join on time for this session with Dr. Barve to have an  opportunity to ask him questions, an opportunity that is only available to the early bird joinees! The Event link will be closed after 15 minutes of starting the event. 

 

In case you are unable to join on time, you can catch the discussion on Youtube Live at: https://youtu.be/AiNyIdZocek or simply search for "Core Counselors IITB" on YouTube. You can also check out other videos on various Mental Health Topics by visiting our YouTube Channel "https://www.youtube.com/c/CoreCounsellorsIITB"

 

Dr Barve will be discussing the mind's reaction to chronic and acute stress, common symptoms of stress that we usually ignore and how beneficial seeking help from professionals can be.

Please share this invitation and information with friends and colleagues at IITB who might be interested in attending this event. We hope that you can join us for this interesting conversation!  See you there! 

Thank you,

About the Speaker:

Dr Rajendra Barve (MD, DPM) is a Consultant Visiting Psychiatrist at IIT Bombay Hospital. He received formal training as a psychiatrist and is an HRD trainer, facilitator by design and is also a media personality. His philosophy embodies Western Medical science and the soul of Eastern wisdom. 

Regards,Core Counselling Team
