Information on COVID-19


We do not have any specific rules on quarantine anymore, except for isolation of anyone who has covid.

In general we follow Govt of Maharashtra and Mumbai municipal corporation rules related to Covid.

Contact Numbers

The following numbers are available 24x7 for reporting any suspected case and for seeking help.

  1. Hospital: 1110

  2. Security: 1100, 1122

General Information
  1. Anyone on campus showing symptoms that may be covid related must contact IIT Hospital for further advice.

  2. Follow the general instructions available from trusted sites ONLY, such as

    • https://www.mygov.in/covid-19
    • http://www.who.int/emergencies/diseases/novel-coronavirus-2019/advice-for-public
